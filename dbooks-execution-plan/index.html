Phase 0Legal FoundationWeek 1-2 · $310

1 Form Texas LLC

  • Go to Texas Secretary of State SOSDirect
  • Search name availability: "DBooks LLC"
  • File Certificate of Formation (Form 205)
  • Entity type: LLC · Registered agent: You, Celina TX · Manager-managed
  • Pay $300 filing fee
  • Receive Certificate of Formation (2-3 business days)

2 Get EIN (Same Day, Free)

  • IRS.gov → LLC → Single Member → Started New Business
  • Receive EIN immediately · Download confirmation PDF

3 Open Business Bank Account (Free)

  • Mercury (recommended, online, free) or Chase Business Complete
  • Bring: LLC Certificate + EIN Letter + Personal ID
RULE: NEVER mix personal and business money

4 Register Domain ($10.44)

  • Cloudflare Registrar → dbookstore.com ($10.44/yr, flat rate)
  • Alternatives: dbooksapp.com, getdbooks.com, dbooks.store

5 Set Up Accounting (Free)

  • Sign up for Wave (free) · Connect bank account
  • Income: Book Sales, App Sales, Subscriptions
  • Expenses: Software, AI APIs, Marketing, Legal
LOG EVERY TRANSACTION from day 1 — tax time will thank you

The 10 Golden Rules

1
Amazon first, own platform second.
Amazon has 300M customers. Use them as your discovery engine.
2
Every Amazon book is a funnel to your app.
That last page promo is gold. Every reader is a potential app user.
3
Don't build features nobody asked for.
Sell books first. Build AI tools later when you have users.
4
Track everything.
Revenue, costs, downloads, reviews. Monthly P&L statement.
5
Write more books = more revenue.
Each book is a recurring digital asset that earns while you sleep.
6
Don't quit your day job.
Build nights and weekends until you hit $5K MRR consistently.
7
Physical copies at meetups = marketing.
Hand someone a real book and they remember you.
8
Reviews are currency.
First 20 Amazon reviews trigger the algorithm. Ask everyone.
9
Email list > social followers.
You OWN your email list. Algorithms can't take it away.
10
$500 startup. $50/month. Infinite upside.
The lowest-risk business you can start. The only cost is your time.
